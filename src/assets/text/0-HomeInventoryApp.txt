Home Inventory Application (Version 1.0) This App helps users efficiently catalog and manage items in their homes for purposes such as organization, insurance documentation, and decluttering. Users can create accounts to securely store and organize inventory details, including item categories, purchase information, and current values. The application also supports photo uploads, enhancing item records with visual documentation. The modular design ensures a smooth user experience and lays the groundwork for easy future updates. The current video demonstrates how simple it is to add items to a personalized inventory collection, with a focus on functionality and user-centric design.
